Lesser Known Campaigners For Indian Independence






Tanguturi Prakasam

Tangaturi Prakasam was the chief mminister of the Madras Presidency, and founded the Zamindari Enquiry Committee, which enquired structural agricultural distortion perpetrated because of the British Government's Zamindari system.

Aruna Asaf Ali

Aruna Asaf Ali was a publisher, political activist and educator, who was known for raising the Indian national flag during the 1942 Quit India Movement at Bombay's Gowalia Tank Maidan. Her brave actions earned her the titles "Grand Old Lady" and "Heroine of 1942"

Gangu Mehtar

Gangu Mehtar was a skilled wrestler, who was a major part of Nana Saheb's army, and he killed around 150 Englishmen singlehandedly.
